11 Neighborhood: neighborhood names represent various physical locations within the city limits of Ames, Iowa

       Bloomington Heights (Blmngtn): A residential neighborhood known for its family-friendly environment and well-maintained homes.

       Bluestem (Blueste):Characterized by newer developments and modern amenities, appealing to young families and professionals.

       Briardale (BrDale):A more established neighborhood with a mix of older and newer homes, offering a suburban feel.

       Brookside (BrkSide):Close to Brookside Park, offering residents easy access to recreational activities and green spaces.

       Clear Creek (ClearCr):Known for its picturesque surroundings and proximity to natural features like creeks and trails.

       College Creek (CollgCr):Popular among students and faculty, located near Iowa State University with convenient access to campus facilities.

       Crawford (Crawfor):A historic neighborhood with charming older homes and a sense of community.

       Edwards (Edwards):Offers a mix of residential and commercial areas, providing diverse living and shopping options.

       Gilbert (Gilbert):A small town feel within the city limits, known for its tight-knit community and local schools.

       Iowa DOT and Rail Road (IDOTRR):Industrial area with a focus on transportation and infrastructure-related businesses.

       Meadow Village (MeadowV):A suburban neighborhood with a variety of housing options and family-friendly amenities.

       Mitchell (Mitchel):Known for its spacious lots and semi-rural atmosphere, offering a quieter living environment.

       North Ames (Names): A residential area with a variety of home styles, close to schools and shopping centers.

       Northridge (NoRidge): Upscale neighborhood with well-maintained homes and proximity to parks and recreational facilities.

       Northpark Villa (NPkVill):Features modern homes and community amenities, popular with young families and retirees.

       Northridge Heights (NridgHt):An extension of Northridge, offering newer homes and upscale living options.

       Northwest Ames (NWAmes):A blend of residential and commercial properties, providing convenient access to various amenities.

       Old Town (OldTown):historic district with charming architecture, close to downtown Ames and its cultural attractions.

       South & West of Iowa State University (SWISU):Primarily student housing and university-related facilities, vibrant with a youthful atmosphere.

       Sawyer (Sawyer):Residential neighborhood with affordable housing options and community parks.

       Sawyer West (SawyerW):Newer development adjacent to Sawyer, offering modern homes and family-friendly amenities.

       Somerset (Somerst):Planned community with a mix of residential and commercial spaces, known for its community-oriented design.

       Stone Brook (StoneBr):A quiet neighborhood with scenic views and access to nature trails and parks.

       Timberland (Timber):Offers larger lots and a rural feel, with an emphasis on privacy and natural surroundings.

       Veenker (Veenker):Named after the Veenker Memorial Golf Course, providing a scenic and recreational living environment.
